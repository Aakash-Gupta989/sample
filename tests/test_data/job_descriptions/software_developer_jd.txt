Adobe is redefining how enterprises harness data for customer experience. As a Software Development Engineer, you’ll be a technical leader driving the evolution of Adobe’s Experience Platform. You’ll architect scalable systems, influence governance strategy, and build tools that empower data-driven innovation across industries.

Why This Role Appeals to Visionaries
* Architectural Ownership: Lead the design of core platform services that support billions of data transactions daily.
* Strategic Impact: Shape Adobe’s data governance and privacy frameworks in collaboration with product and legal teams.
* Innovation at Scale: Build intelligent systems that support real-time personalization, AI-driven insights, and secure data exchange.
* Thought Leadership: Mentor engineers and represent Adobe in technical forums.
What You’ll Build
* Distributed microservices for data ingestion, transformation, and governance.
* Metadata-driven APIs that support lineage tracking, policy enforcement, and federated access control.
* AI-enhanced developer tools for compliance automation and anomaly detection.
What You Bring
* 4+ years of experience in backend or platform engineering, preferably in cloud-native environments.
* Expertise in Java, or Python; experience with distributed systems.
* Passion for building resilient, scalable, and secure systems.
* Strong communication and leadership skills; ability to influence across teams.
